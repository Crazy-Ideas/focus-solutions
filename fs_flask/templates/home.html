About Us

Welcome to Focus Solutions, your number one source for all the data you require of Competition Hotels. We're dedicated to providing you with the most accurate Reader Board data of hotels, with a focus on dependability, accuracy and uniqueness.

Founded in 2009 by Punit Shah, Focus Solutions has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office in Andheri, Mumbai. When Focus Solutions first started out at as his passion for data collection and providing the same to hotels; so as to help hotels provide better service and get to their key clientele.

Reader Board services help hotels to compare and understand the market scenario and assists their marketing needs.

The Reader Board Activity was first started in 2002 and was well known in the hotel industry as the “Ikon Report”. When partners decided to move on their own paths, Punit Shah took over the Reader Board Activity under Focus Solutions from 2009 onwards. Focus Solutions provides services to cities across India.

We have been changing the ways we do our business and have embraced modern technologies. We are happy to introduce Focus Banquet Reports, an online platform that provides accurate banquet information to all hotels in India with rich set of reporting features.

We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
